## 1. Problem Statement
Millions of citizens across India are unable to access government schemes and public services due to language barriers, low digital literacy, fragmented information systems, and complex eligibility processes.

## 2. Objective
To build a voice-first, multilingual AI assistant that enables citizens to discover, understand, and apply for relevant schemes and services through conversational interaction.

## 3. Target Users
- Rural citizens
- Students
- Farmers
- Women beneficiaries
- Senior citizens
- Low-literacy populations

## 4. Functional Requirements
- Automatic language detection
- Voice-based interaction (IVR + WhatsApp)
- Eligibility assessment engine
- Scheme recommendation system
- Step-by-step application guidance
- Personalized document checklist
- Deadline reminder notifications
- Location-based service center mapping
- Human escalation option

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- Low-bandwidth optimization
- Scalability across India
- Secure data handling
- High availability
- Multilingual support

## 6. Success Metrics
- Increased scheme adoption rate
- Reduced application errors
- User engagement metrics
- Rural adoption rate
